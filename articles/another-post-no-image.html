Another Post Without an Image

September 23, 2025

This article serves as a demonstration of the new placeholder image functionality. The main JavaScript on the homepage will detect that this article file does not contain an tag and will automatically generate a colorful placeholder instead.

This ensures that the layout of the homepage remains consistent and visually appealing, even for posts that don't have a dedicated header image. The placeholder will even include the title of this post!
